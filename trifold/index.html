Our Story

Chammy's Equine Sanctuary is a haven for horses, mini horses, ponies, and donkeys. Since 2015, Angela—our President and sole staff member—has voluntarily cared for these animals while working full-time as a public school teacher.

In 2024, the Chammy's Equine Sanctuary Foundation was established to support Angela's mission and ensure its long-term sustainability, with a dedicated board providing stewardship and governance.

Our Vision

A world where every equine—horse, pony, mini, or donkey—lives free from neglect and suffering. We rescue, rehabilitate, and advocate—and we work to stop neglect before it starts through education and community outreach.

Meet a Few Residents

Cowboy Rescued

A friend's plea brought Cowboy to us—underweight and struggling to breathe. With Dr. B's breathing program, he's now thriving two years on.

Flower In Memory

Saved from a Kansas kill pen by strangers who couldn't walk away. She spent six gentle years in her pink fly boots before we said goodbye in 2022.

Charlie Thriving

Two years old, beat up, and scared in an Oklahoma feedlot. Today, a beloved 12-year-old hunter/jumper sailing over fences with joy.

Rio Favorite

A mini with a crooked leg and a shattered hind leg from a kick—put back together by Dr. B and now the loudest personality in the barn.

The Donkeys 10 Years

Slaughter-bound a decade ago. Today, kings of the pasture—peaceful, well-fed, and absolutely in charge.
